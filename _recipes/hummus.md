---

layout: recipe
title: "Bridgette's Hummus"
image: hummus.jpg

ingredients:
- 1 can chickpeas
- mint 1tsp
- coriander 1tsp
- paprika 1tsp
- pinch rosemary
- 1tsp turmeric
- cumin 1/2 tsp
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 1/2 tbsp tahini
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 1/2 tbsp lemon juice

directions:
- fry chickpeas and other ingredients, aside from lemon and water
- once cooked, move to a bowl.
- add lemon juice water as well as more olive oil and tahini to taste. 
- Blend with a stick blender until smooth.
